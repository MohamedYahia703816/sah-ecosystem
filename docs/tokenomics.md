# $SAH Tokenomics

## Overview

$SAH is a **utility and governance token** on BNB Chain, designed to power the SAH (Sonic Architect Hub) ecosystem — a Web3 AI music production platform. Unlike speculative tokens, $SAH derives its value from **real utility**: every AI service, every tool, every feature requires $SAH to use.

## Utility — What Can You Do With $SAH?

### 1. Access AI Music Tools
Every service on the SAH platform costs $SAH:
- **Stem Separation** — Isolate vocals, drums, bass, instruments
- **Audio Restoration** — Remove noise, echo, artifacts
- **MIDI Transcription** — Convert audio to MIDI files
- **Vocal Synthesis** — Generate realistic vocals from text
- **Smart Mastering** — One-click professional mastering
- **Global Distribution** — Upload to Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music

### 2. Earn Through Tasks
Complete daily tasks in the Mini App to earn SAH:
- **Daily Check-in** — 10 SAH
- **Referral Bonus** — 400 SAH per referral
- **Task Completion** — Variable rewards
- **Leaderboard Rankings** — Weekly bonus pools

### 3. Governance
$SAH holders vote on:
- **Burn decisions** — How much supply to burn annually (up to 5%)
- **Feature prioritization** — Which AI tools to build next
- **Treasury allocation** — How to spend the 20% treasury
- **Partnership approvals** — New integrations and collaborations

### 4. Deflationary Mechanism
- **5% Annual Burn Cap** — Maximum 50M SAH burned per year
- **Service Fee Burns** — A portion of every service fee is burned
- **Community-Driven** — Holders vote on burn amounts and timing
- **Transparent** — All burns recorded on-chain

## Economic Model

### Revenue Flow
```
User pays $SAH for AI service
    ↓
70% → Service Provider (platform revenue)
20% → Treasury (community fund)
10% → Burn (permanent removal from supply)
```

### Demand Drivers
| Source | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **AI Tool Usage** | Every stem separation, restoration, mastering costs SAH |
| **Distribution Fees** | Uploading to streaming platforms requires SAH |
| **Premium Features** | Advanced tools and higher quality outputs cost more |
| **Governance Participation** | Staking SAH for voting power |

### Supply Controls
| Mechanism | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **Annual Burn** | Reduces total supply by up to 5% per year |
| **Max Wallet** | Prevents whale accumulation (2% cap) |
| **Max TX** | Prevents large dumps (1% cap per transaction) |
| **Vesting Schedules** | Team and investor tokens locked for 2+ years |
