# Getting Started with SAH

## Welcome to Sonic Architect Hub

SAH is where **music creation meets blockchain economy**. Whether you're a musician, producer, investor, or just curious about AI music — there's something for you.

## Step 1: Join the Community

### Open the Telegram Mini App
1. Open Telegram and search for **@Sah_Sonic_bot**
2. Tap **Start** to begin
3. You'll receive **400 SAH** as an early adopter reward
4. Tap **🎵 Open Studio** to enter the Mini App

### What You'll See
- **Your Balance** — SAH tokens you've earned
- **Profit/Hour** — How many SAH you earn passively
- **Your Studio** — Your music production workspace
- **Tasks** — Daily activities to earn more SAH

## Step 3: Use AI Music Tools

### Upload Your Audio
1. Send any audio file to **@Sah_Sonic_bot**
2. Choose the tool you want to use:
   - 🎚️ **Stem Separation** — Split into vocals, drums, bass, etc.
   - ✨ **Audio Restoration** — Clean up noise and artifacts
   - 🎹 **MIDI Transcription** — Convert to MIDI files

### Get Your Results
- Processing takes 30 seconds to 2 minutes
- Results are sent back as downloadable files
- Each tool costs a small amount of SAH

## Step 5: Join the Referral System

### What Is It?
A community-driven growth system where members earn by participating in a structured network. Read the full [Referral System Guide](referral-system.md).

### How to Join
1. Pay **$3** entry fee (supports the ecosystem)
2. Get placed in a table of 7 members
3. Earn as new members join through your invitation
4. Exit and re-enter to start new cycles

### Earning Potential
- **Minimum:** $3 returned on entry
- **Average:** $30-300 per cycle
- **Maximum:** ~$2,187 at full tree depth
